TIP 1 Set Spending Limits

EEncourage users to set specific spending limits for different categories like groceries, dining out, entertainment, and more. Establishing these limits can aid them in adhering to their budget and fostering greater mindfulness regarding their expenses. Incorporate a convenient feature enabling users to directly input their allocated budget for each category on the expense form..

TIP 2 Track Daily Expenses

Suggest users track their daily expenses to identify patterns and areas where they can cut back. Provide a section on the expense form where users can enter additional details about their daily expenses, such as the item purchased, cost, and category. This can help users visualize their spending habits and make informed decisions about where to cut expenses.

TIP 3 Compare Prices - Shop Smart

Encourage users to compare prices and shop smart to get the best deals on their purchases. You can include a feature that allows users to input the price of an item and compare it with prices from other retailers or online stores. Additionally, provide tips on how to save money while shopping, such as using coupons, buying in bulk, or waiting for sales.
